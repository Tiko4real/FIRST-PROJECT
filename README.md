## User Experience (UX)

Simplicity: The website follows a clean and minimalist design approach, ensuring that users can easily find the information they need without distractions.

Consistency: Consistent use of colors, typography, and layout throughout the website provides a cohesive experience for users, helping them navigate seamlessly between different sections.

Accessibility: The website is designed to be accessible to all users, including those with disabilities. It incorporates alt text for images

### User Stories

* First-time visitor goals

   * Understand the main purpose of the site and learn more about me.
   * Easily navigate throughout the site.
   * Discover my competencies and areas of expertise.
   * View my career journey and accomplishments.
   * Find contact information to reach out to me.
   * Access my social media profiles for further engagement.

* Returning visitor goals

   * Explore my latest projects and career updates.
   * Revisit my competencies and refresh knowledge.
   * Easily find contact information for follow-up inquiries.

 * Frequent user goals
    * Revisit specific sections of the website, such as the career highlights for reference or inspiration.

## Features

   * The website aims to provide users with insights into my career and competencies.
   * Users can easily explore my portfolio by navigating to the career showcase section and accessing detailed project information.
   * Responsive design ensures optimal viewing experience across all device sizes, enhancing accessibility for users.
   * Contact information is readily available, allowing users to reach out to me for inquiries, collaborations, or feedback.
   * Social media integration enables users to connect with me on various platforms, fostering engagement and networking opportunities.
   * Language selection feature accommodates users from diverse linguistic backgrounds, offering a personalized browsing experience.
